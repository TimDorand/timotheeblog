Stick Together a radio show about solidarity in the city

The main goal is to share stories about solidarity in Berlin, give simple examples on how to help and have meaningful activities.

Following the workshop in Amsterdam at the TNP Radio event, I felt like I had to jump on the opportunity to have a positive impact around me using the web radio airwaves and community in Berlin.

That’s how Pacaline, Roxanne and I started the podcast called Stick Together. In each episode, we focus on a problem in the city and will present one of the many solutions to solve it. Everytime we will be joined by a guest engaged in an organisation that will answer our questions. The main goal is to share stories about solidarity in Berlin, give simple examples on how to help and have meaningful activities. Overall, the show tries to have a positive impact on its surrounding.
